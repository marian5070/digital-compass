title: I got scammed buying something online. What do I do?
summary: You paid on OLX (Romania's biggest classifieds site), an online shop or a social network and the product isn't coming, or the seller has disappeared. Here's what to do to get your money back and where to complain.
situation: I paid online and the product isn't coming or the seller has disappeared
icon: "🛒"
order: 3
severity: important
category: cumparaturi-bani
lastReviewed: "9 July 2026"
steps:
  - "<strong>Gather the evidence now:</strong> screenshots of the listing, the conversation, the payment page, the transfer confirmation and the seller's details. Don't delete anything."
  - "If you paid by <strong>card</strong>, call your bank and ask about a <strong>\"chargeback\"</strong> (getting the payment back for a product you never received). Your chances are better if you act fast."
  - "If you paid by <strong>bank transfer</strong> to an account, ask your bank to try a payment recall — sometimes there's still time if the money hasn't been withdrawn."
  - "Report the listing and the seller on the platform where you bought (OLX, Facebook Marketplace etc.)."
  - "File a <strong>complaint with ANPC</strong> (Romania's consumer protection authority — for shops/traders) and a <strong>report with the Police (Poliția Română)</strong> (for fraud)."
donts:
  - "Do not send any more money \"to release the parcel\" or \"for customs fees\" — that's the scam continuing."
  - "Do not accept a \"refund\" through a link that asks for your card details again: it's the second trap."
  - "Do not give up on the evidence — without screenshots, a report is much harder to back up."
recognize:
  - "<strong>A price far too low</strong> for the market, with pressure to pay quickly \"because there are other buyers too\"."
  - "The seller takes you off the platform (onto WhatsApp) and asks for <strong>payment in advance</strong> by direct transfer."
  - "A new account, no reviews, photos taken from the internet, refuses cash on delivery or meeting in person."
report:
  - channel: "ANPC (Romania's consumer protection authority)"
    detail: "For problems with online shops and traders. File a complaint with your evidence."
    url: "https://anpc.ro"
  - channel: "Your bank"
    detail: "Ask about a chargeback (card) or a payment recall (transfer). Call the official number on your card."
  - channel: "The Police (Poliția Română)"
    detail: "File a report for fraud. Take all your evidence with you."
    url: "https://www.politiaromana.ro"
  - channel: "The platform (OLX / Marketplace)"
    detail: "Report the listing and the seller, so nobody else gets cheated."

**First, breathe.** Even if you've already paid, not everything is lost: if you used a card, your bank can try to get the payment back, and the evidence you gather now matters enormously. First thing — don't send any more money.
