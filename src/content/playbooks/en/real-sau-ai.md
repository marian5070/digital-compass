title: I can't tell if it's real or made with AI. What do I do?
summary: You received a photo, a clip, a voice message or a video of a public figure that seems fake. Here's how to recognise AI-made content (deepfakes) and what to do before you react.
situation: I received something that seems fake or made with AI
icon: "🎭"
order: 7
severity: important
lastReviewed: "9 July 2026"
steps:
  - "<strong>Don't react in the heat of the moment.</strong> Don't send money, don't give out details and don't share it before checking. That rush is exactly what the people behind this want."
  - "Check the source on an <strong>official, separate channel</strong>: if it's \"your bank/an institution\", go to their official website yourself; if it's a relative, call them on the number you already know."
  - "Look for the original: do a search by image or by the exact phrase in the message and see if it appears on reputable news sources."
  - "For voice messages or a \"relative in danger\": agree on a <strong>family password question</strong> that only you know, and use it on the phone when something seems urgent."
  - "If it's being used to defraud or blackmail you, report the content on the platform and to the authorities."
donts:
  - "Don't send money or details based on a clip, voice message or video, no matter how convincing it seems."
  - "Don't share it until you've checked — you could end up spreading a fake yourself."
  - "Don't let yourself be rushed by \"act now, it's urgent\"."
recognize:
  - "<strong>Public figures \"recommending\" investments:</strong> clips of Ion Țiriac (a famous Romanian businessman), Mugur Isărescu / BNR (Romania's central bank governor) or politicians praising a platform. They are deepfakes — see also the playbook about investments."
  - "<strong>In video:</strong> lips out of sync with the voice, rare blinking, edges or background that \"shimmer\", odd hands/ears/teeth."
  - "<strong>In voice:</strong> flat intonation, unnatural pauses, no breathing sounds, an \"emergency\" demanding money right away."
  - "A new account, few followers, comments turned off, a link that quickly takes you off the platform."

  - channel: "The Police (Poliția Română)"
    detail: "If you were defrauded or blackmailed, file a report with your evidence (screenshots, links)."
    url: "https://www.politiaromana.ro"

**First, breathe.** AI can imitate a face or a voice today, but it can't imitate a **simple check**. Almost any deepfake falls apart if you give yourself a few minutes and confirm through another, official channel. Rushing you is their only real weapon.
