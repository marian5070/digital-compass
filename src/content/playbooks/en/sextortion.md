**First, breathe. It is not your fault and you are not alone.** The people who do this rely on fear, shame and haste — so you pay before you can think clearly. But paying doesn't free you; it encourages them. Your power is to not react, keep the evidence and ask for help. You are the victim here, not the guilty one.
